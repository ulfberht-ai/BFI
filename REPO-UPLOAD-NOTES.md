- `README.md`
- `LICENSE`
- `PATENT-PENDING.md`
- `REPO-UPLOAD-NOTES.md`
- `scripts/`
- `docs/`
- `data/`
- `results/`

Suggested upload sequence:

1. Upload the four root markdown/text files.
2. Upload the full `scripts/` directory.
3. Upload `docs/experiment-protocol.md`.
4. Upload `data/retest_manifest_2026-04-13.json`.
5. Upload the entire `results/` directory, especially the two zip archives and the inventory markdown.

The `results/` folder is already structured to avoid exploding 1,000+ generated files directly into the repo. Keep the archives zipped unless you intentionally want the raw corpus browsable file-by-file.
